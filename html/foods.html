    Asparagus

    Vegetable

    Your cat might enjoy a small serving of steamed, chopped asparagus once in a while. Make sure it is cooked and cooled.

    Broccoli

    Vegetable

    Broccoli is considered non-toxic and can be served steamed and cooled to cats. Remember to only feed this as a rare treat and in small quantities.

    Carrots

    Vegetable

    Cats can definitely eat steamed carrots. They are high in fiber and packed with a bunch of yummy nutrition.

    Green Beans

    Vegetable

    Green beans are considered safe for cats, and they are a good source of fiber, protein, and healthy vitamins.

    Pumpkin

    Vegetable

    Pumpkin is a great source of fiber and vitamins for your cat. Pumpkin can also help with issues around constipation or even complications surrounding impactions due to hairballs.

    Spinach

    Vegetable

    Cats naturally like leafy greens, and spinach carries quite a few beneficial vitamins for your cat.

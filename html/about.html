    Welcome to this cat website, a place where cat lovers can come together to learn, share and connect with other feline enthusiasts from all around the world. This website is dedicated to all things cats, from their adorable antics to their unique personalities and everything in between. On this website, you might see a few cat breeds that are famous and can be seen daily around the world, as well as a few foods like vegetable, wet, and dry cat food. I also use a few toys that have high ratings when it comes to feeding and entertaining cats.
    As of 2023, The International Cat Association (TICA) recognizes 73 standardized breeds, the Cat Fanciers' Association (CFA) recognizes 45, the Fédération Internationale Féline (FIFe) recognizes 50, the Governing Council of the Cat Fancy (GCCF) recognizes 45, and the World Cat Federation (WCF) recognizes 69.

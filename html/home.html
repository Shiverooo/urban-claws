    • What is cats?

    The cat is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal.
    It is only domesticated species in family Felidae and commonly referred to
    the domestic cat or house cat.

    About cats

    Cats possess other features of their wild relatives being basically carnivorous,
    remarkably agile and powerful, and finely coordinated in movement.

    Origin and history of cats

    The ancestor of modern domestic cats was the last to appear,
    about 3.4 million years ago. A small wildcat species was first domesticated
    in Near East 8,000 - 10,000 years ago.
